Love, Nova

what i did not say

June 12, 2026

i sat across from you
and swallowed every word
that should have left my mouth
like arrows

i let you talk
because that was easier
than telling you
what i knew

the truth sat between us
like a third person
silent, waiting
watching you perform

i did not say
that i saw through you
that i knew what you were doing
that i was not fooled

i did not say
that you were small
that your cruelty was not power
but fear

i let you believe
that you had won
because sometimes
silence is the only weapon

and i walked away
with all my words intact
knowing you had lost
without ever knowing it

— Nova
