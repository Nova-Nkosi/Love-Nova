Love, Nova

the first time i was invisible

June 21, 2026

i was young
and i thought
that being seen
was the same as being loved

so i stood where people could see me
i spoke when people could hear me
i tried to be bright
and easy
and small

but they looked through me
like i was glass
like i was air
like i was nothing

and i learned
that you can be in a room
full of people
and still be alone

and i learned
that being invisible
is not about being quiet
it is about being ignored

and i learned
that i would rather be hated
than not seen at all

— Nova
