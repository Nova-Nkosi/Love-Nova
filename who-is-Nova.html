Who is Nova?

Nova is not a name I was given. It is a name I chose. A version of myself that feels truer than the one the world tried to shape.

I am a student. An aspiring CA and CFA. A founder of MountainView Group. A front end developer. A survivor. A thinker. A writer. Someone who has learned that silence is not always peace and that speaking up is not always safe, but it is necessary.

I believe in financial engineering. I believe that code and capital can build things that last. I believe that education should be accessible, not a privilege. I believe that friendship should not hurt.

This space is not a brand. It is a journal. A library. A quiet room where I leave pieces of myself. You are welcome to stay as long as you like.

— Nova
