Love, Nova

the shape of you

June 24, 2026

i did not know
that i was waiting
until you arrived

not waiting for someone
but waiting to be seen
the way you see me

you look at me
like i am not broken
like the pieces i hide
are not shameful
but sacred

you do not flinch
at the weight i carry
you do not ask me
to be lighter
you just sit beside me
and let me be heavy

and somehow
that makes it lighter

i have spent so long
being too much
too quiet
too loud
too complicated
too hard to love

but you
you never said that
you just stayed

you stayed when i could not speak
you stayed when i could not smile
you stayed when i was sure
you would leave

and you are still here

i do not know
how to thank you
for that
so i will just say this

you are the first person
who made me believe
that being known
is not the same
as being unsafe

that love
is not a trap
but a door

that i can walk through
without fear

i am learning
how to let you in
slowly
carefully
one wall at a time

and you are patient
you are kind
you are the quiet
i did not know i needed

i love you
not because you are perfect
but because you see me
and you stay

that is everything

— Nova
