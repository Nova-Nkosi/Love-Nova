Love, Nova

the weight of it

June 15, 2026

some things do not leave
they just sit
in the back of your chest
like stones

you learn to breathe around them
to move
to smile
to pretend they are not there

but at night
when the world is quiet
you feel them
all of them

the things you carried
the things you buried
the things you told no one
because no one asked

and you wonder
if they will ever be light
or if you will just learn
to carry them better

— Nova
